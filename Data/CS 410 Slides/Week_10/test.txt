7 Why Text Categorization? To enrich text representation (more understanding of text) Text can now be represented in multiple levels (keywords + categories) Semantic categories assigned can be directly or indirectly useful for an application Semantic categories facilitate aggregation of text content (e.g., aggregating all positive/negative opinions about a product) To infer properties of entities associated with text data (discovery of knowledge about the world ) As long as an entity can be associated with text data, we can always use the text data to help categorize the associated entities E.g., discovery of non -native speakers of a language; prediction of party affiliation based on a political speech
